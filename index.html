Origins

Born in the underground data havens of Neo-Shanghai during the third decade after the Collapse. Orphaned during a data raid, Aria was taken in by the secretive Order of the Digital Veil.

Awakening

At age 16, during a deep excavation of pre-Collapse archives, Aria accidentally triggered an ancient security protocol that fused with her neural implants. This unexpected merger awakened latent abilities to perceive and manipulate the underlying code that forms reality — the mythical Arcane Syntax.

The Great Hunt

Now hunted by both corporate data miners and shadowy government agencies, Aria travels between secure nodes in the global network, searching for fragments of the legendary Ethereal Algorithm while offering her unique skills to those who can aid her quest.

Nexus Protocol

Recently inducted into the secretive Nexus Protocol — an alliance of tech-mages and digital archeologists working to prevent another Collapse by recovering and safeguarding ancient knowledge.
